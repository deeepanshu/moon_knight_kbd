## Sea-Picro EXT Pinout

### Left Side
- **D+** - USB Data Positive
- **0** - TX (UART)
- **1** - RX (UART)
- **GND** - Ground
- **GND** - Ground
- **2** - SDA (I2C)
- **3** - SCL (I2C)
- **4** - GPIO
- **5** - GPIO
- **6** - GPIO
- **7** - GPIO
- **8** - GPIO
- **9** - GPIO

### Right Side
- **D-** - USB Data Negative
- **RAW** - Raw power input
- **GND** - Ground
- **nRST** - Reset (active low)
- **3V3** - 3.3V output
- **29** - ADC3
- **28** - ADC2
- **27** - ADC1
- **26** - ADC0
- **22** - SCK (SPI)
- **20** - MISO (SPI)
- **23** - MOSI (SPI)
- **21** - nCS (SPI)

### Bottom Pads
- **12, 13, 14, 15, 16** - Extended GPIO
- **25** - 5V RGB output
